[Joplin Cloud](https://joplinapp.org/plans/) is a web service designed specifically for Joplin. In addition to syncing your data across devices, it lets you publish individual notes to the internet and share entire notebooks with friends, family, or colleagues. Compared to other sync targets, Joplin Cloud also includes performance improvements that make synchronisation faster.

## Connect Joplin to Joplin Cloud

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Joplin Cloud account">
    Visit [joplinapp.org/plans](https://joplinapp.org/plans/) and sign up for an account. A free trial is available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Configuration screen">
    In the Joplin desktop or mobile app, open the **Configuration** screen (gear icon or **Tools > Options** on desktop).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Joplin Cloud as the sync target">
    Go to the **Synchronisation** section and select **Joplin Cloud** from the list of sync targets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your Joplin Cloud **email address** and **password**, then click **Apply**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Synchronise">
    Click **Synchronise** in the sidebar to start your first sync. All your notes will be uploaded to Joplin Cloud and will become available on any other device where you sign in with the same account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Make sure you do not forget your password. For security reasons, it cannot be recovered if lost.
</Note>

## Sharing a notebook

You can share an entire notebook with other Joplin Cloud users. When a notebook is shared, invited users can see all the notes inside it. Changes sync to all participants.

To share a notebook, right-click it in the sidebar and select **Share notebook**. You can invite users by their Joplin Cloud email address.

## Publishing a note

Publishing makes a note accessible to anyone with the link, without requiring a Joplin account. Published notes are read-only for viewers.

To publish a note, right-click it and select **Publish note**. Joplin will generate a public URL you can copy and share.

<Warning>
  Published notes are publicly accessible by anyone with the URL. Do not publish notes containing sensitive or private information.
</Warning>
