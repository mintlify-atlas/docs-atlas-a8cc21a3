The Joplin mobile app is available for Android and iOS (iPhone and iPad). It shares the same note format and sync capabilities as the desktop and terminal apps, so your notes are always up to date across all your devices.

## Installation

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Play Store">
        Install Joplin from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.cozic.joplin&utm_source=GitHub&utm_campaign=README&pcampaignid=MKT-Other-global-all-co-prtnr-py-PartBadge-Mar2515-1).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Install">
        Tap **Install** and wait for the download to complete.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open Joplin">
        Tap **Open** in the Play Store, or find the Joplin icon in your app drawer.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>You can also install Joplin by downloading the [APK file](https://objects.joplinusercontent.com/v3.5.8/joplin-v3.5.8.apk?source=JoplinWebsite&type=New) directly. To do so, enable **Install unknown apps** in your Android settings for the app you use to open the file.</Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the App Store">
        Install Joplin from the [App Store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/joplin/id1315599797).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Get">
        Tap **Get** and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open Joplin">
        Tap **Open** in the App Store, or find the Joplin icon on your home screen.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first note">
    Tap the **+** button to create a new note. Notes are written in Markdown and rendered in the viewer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organise into notebooks">
    Tap the menu icon to access your notebooks. Create a new notebook to group related notes together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up synchronisation (optional)">
    Go to **Configuration > Synchronisation** and choose a sync target — Joplin Cloud, Nextcloud, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV. Once configured, Joplin syncs automatically in the background.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Offline-first" icon="wifi-slash">
    All your notes are stored locally on your device. You can read, create, and edit notes without an internet connection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-device sync" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Sync with the desktop and terminal apps via Joplin Cloud, Nextcloud, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV.
  </Card>
  <Card title="End-to-end encryption" icon="lock">
    Enable E2EE in **Configuration > Encryption** to ensure synced data is encrypted and only readable by you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Markdown support" icon="pen-to-square">
    Write in Markdown with a live preview, or use the built-in rich-text editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notebooks and tags" icon="folder">
    Organise notes into notebooks and tag them for flexible access across your library.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attachments" icon="paperclip">
    Attach photos, files, and other resources to your notes directly from your device.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
