Joplin is an offline-first note-taking application that keeps your data private. Your notes live on your device — sync is optional and always encrypted end-to-end. With support for Markdown, notebooks, tags, and a powerful plugin system, Joplin adapts to any workflow.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Joplin">
    Visit the [installation page](/installation) and download the app for your operating system. Desktop, mobile (Android and iOS), and terminal versions are available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first note">
    Open Joplin and press **Ctrl+N** (or **Cmd+N** on macOS) to create a new note. Notes are written in Markdown and rendered in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organise with notebooks and tags">
    Group related notes into [notebooks](/apps/notebooks-and-tags) and apply tags to cross-reference notes across different notebooks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up synchronisation (optional)">
    Connect Joplin to [Joplin Cloud](/sync/joplin-cloud), Nextcloud, Dropbox, OneDrive, or any WebDAV service to sync your notes across devices with [end-to-end encryption](/sync/e2ee).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Joplin?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Privacy first" icon="lock">
    All synced data is encrypted end-to-end. Only you hold the keys — not Joplin, not the sync provider.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Works offline" icon="wifi-slash">
    Joplin is offline-first. All your notes are stored locally and accessible without an internet connection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open source" icon="github">
    Joplin is fully open source under the AGPL license. Inspect the code, contribute, or self-host your sync server.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-platform" icon="laptop-mobile">
    Available for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS — all apps sync seamlessly with each other.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extensible" icon="puzzle-piece">

  </Card>
